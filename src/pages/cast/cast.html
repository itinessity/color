Raphael Nicholas
Lead actor • Albert
Raphael Nicholas is a bilingual actor, British/German who played in over 50 Productions in the last 12 years. He is based in Vienna and Munic and was raised in Bavaria on a farm. Besides his physical training in fencing, stage fighting and horse riding. He also is a sing and songwriter and plays the piano and saxophone.
Shay Denison
Lead actor • Grace
Shay Denison is an actress best known for Like Dogs (2021), A Carolina Christmas (2020), Tammy (2020), and Crystal City (2019). Since graduating Summa Cum Laude from Emerson College with a BFA in Acting and a minor in Business Studies, she has worked on four feature films, three commercials, three television shows, and more than twelve short films. In 2021, Shay Denison booked a lead role in the new series The Banished, which is set to film in Europe in 2024.
